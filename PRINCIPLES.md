Principles

Build from first principles.

Seek understanding before memorization.

⸻

Find the abstraction.

Learning begins by identifying the compression algorithm that explains an entire domain.

⸻

Simplicity beats complexity.

Simple systems are easier to understand, maintain and improve.

⸻

Reveal complexity progressively.

Expose sophistication only when it creates value.

⸻

Specifications before implementation.

Clear thinking precedes good software.

⸻

Decisions over information.

The objective is better decisions, not more data.

⸻

Preserve optionality.

Avoid unnecessary commitments.

Maintain strategic flexibility.

⸻

Build assets.

Prefer creating systems that continue producing value over trading time for money.

⸻

Compound knowledge.

Capture ideas once.

Reuse them many times.

⸻

Measure reality.

Test assumptions.

Update beliefs.

Prefer evidence over intuition.

⸻

Human + AI.

Design systems where AI amplifies human judgment rather than replacing it.

⸻

Think in systems.

Optimize the whole rather than individual parts.
